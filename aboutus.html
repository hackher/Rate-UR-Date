About Us

Rate UR Date was founded in Febuary of 2016 at a Rutgers University hackathon called HackHERS. Five university students sat down for practically 24 hours straight and went to work saving their fellow Scarlet Knights from possible dating disasters. Our various rating categories and scales will show whether someone is worth your valuable Saturday night.

How to Use Rate UR Date

Sign up with us and create your own profile. From there, you can send a "date request". A date request allows for you to give the person you went out with a proper review. We've set up categories for you in order to make the process easier. Review as many people as you've went out with to give your classmates an easier time with picking and choosing!
